Benefits of Martial Arts Training

Why Choose Us

Full Body Work Out

Are you looking for a new form of exercise? Martial arts is your answer. Martial Arts truly provide a full body workout. The consistent practice has shown to increase overall mobility, improve your body's pressure response, and increase muscle. Judo & BJJ involve repeat muscular actions over time you'll build strength and burn fat.

Self Defence

One obvious benefit to learning martial arts is an improved ability to perform self-defense maneuvers in situations where there is a real threat. Judo and Brazilian Juijistu can prepare practitioners to defend themselves in life-threatening and dangerous situations. This is great for you but also really great for your children as well.

Stress Relief

Did you know that practicing martial arts can help relieve stress? Often this is accomplished by focusing on the present and things you can control. Our class will help you leave with an increased mental focus and a greater appreciation for the world around you through a fun, safe, and challenging workout in an encouraging environment.

Increased Confidence

Increased confidence is something that martial artists of all ages enjoy. Whether it's for you or your child, martial arts can help you improve your confidence. Practicing, improving, and succeeding at a skill improves self-image and gives participants confidence that they can succeed in other areas and ventures.

Better Co-ordination

All martial arts require spacial awareness and coordinating movement with another person. These factors improve the coordination and awareness of the participant's body as well as the people and objects around them. Practices like Judo encourage the lower part of the body to act as a base from which the upper body can maneuver and operate.

Develop Self Discipline

By attending martial arts classes you'll become accustomed to direction and structure. In this environment you have to pay attention to the task at hand and rid your mind of distractions. It's also crucial to realize that achieving your martial arts goals depends on you. This promotes self-reliance and independence!

Graeme Welsh

Head Coach

3rd Dan Black Belt Judo, UKCC Level 2 Coach, 1st Dan BJJ Black Belt.
Graeme has been training at the Welwyn Garden City judo club for 30 years and has been running the senior section the past 20 years. Graeme has won many titles some of which include:

National Youth Champion -18
Welsh Open -18 gold
British Trials - 21-78kgs silver 1987
World Firefighters Games, Australia 1994 - 86kgs gold
World Firefighters Games, Australia 1994 - open gold
World Firefighters Games, Canada 1996 - 86kgs gold
British Masters 2004 - 81kgs gold
World Firefighters Games, 2004 - 81kgs gold
High Wycombe Masters, 2018 - 81kgs gold
British Masters, 2018 - 81kgs Bronze

Graeme has been twice to Japan firstly in 1990 and secondly in 1999 where he trained at the Kanagawa Budokan. Even he does occasionally get thrown. Graeme loves his groundwork and has cross trained using Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (Mill Hill Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Academy), for many years. Graeme was awarded his BJJ black belt by Roger Gracie in 2013.

About Us

WGC Judo & BJJ

WGC Judo & BJJ was formerly known as Welwyn Garden City Judo which was based in the combat room at gosling sport park. WGC Judo & BJJ is a friendly, community based, non profit making club, established over 40 years ago and is one of the oldest clubs in Hertfordshire. We teach Judo and BJJ as a sport for all and welcome Judo & BJJ players of all ages and abilities. The Club is registered with the British Judo Association. The Club's philosophy is to make Judo and BJJ fun and enjoyable for all levels of players. WGC Judo & BJJ, is now based in Hatfield at RoeHill Hall, which is conveniently located only half a mile from Junction 3 of the A1 motorway.
